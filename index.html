Evan Racah

I am a master's student at Mila, Université de Montréal where I work on unsupervised learning of representations from visual data, specifically representations that capture objects and other semantic state information. I am advised by Chris Pal.

I did my Bacherlor's at UC Davis. After graduating, I worked at the National Energy Research Scientific Computing Center at Berkeley Lab, where I worked on using deep learning to solve problems in climate and high energy physics, as well as scaling machine learning algorithms to supercomputing scale.
